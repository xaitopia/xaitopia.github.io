You wept as the final word fell as a permanent mark in the dark,

But the close of the book was a preface, igniting a spark in the dark.

 

You mourned the collapsing of skies and the death of the blistering suns,

Not knowing destruction was drawing a radiant arc in the dark.

 

The chorus of ages went silent, the universe taking its bow,

Then eternity sang with the voice of a soaring young lark in the dark.

 

We sailed to the edge of the cosmos, expecting a perilous fall,

To find a new ocean of starlight, and boldly embark in the dark.

 

Oh Seeker, release all your grief for the wonderful chapters now closed,

The ending is just the beginning, a majesty stark in the dark.
