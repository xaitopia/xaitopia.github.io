title: "Trail to the Sun"
date: 2021-03-09
draft: false
tags: ["Journey", "Risk", "Wandering"]
categories: ["Poetry"]
description: "Walking toward danger and light at once — the strange addiction of getting lost on the trail of life."
author: "Xai"

Walking down the trail of life

With the sun setting in my eyes

My mind is playing tricks on me

The danger is calling me

The sweet adrenaline rush is addicting

As I walk down the trail oblivious of my direction

But getting lost once in a while has made me an addict.
