---
title: "A Thousand Battles"
date: 2021-06-09
draft: false
tags: ["Faith", "Resilience", "Peace"]
categories: ["Poetry"]
description: "On the faith that outlasts a thousand rages — the peace that storms cannot reach."
author: "Xai"

The rage from a thousand battles,

Could not keep my peace of mind at bay.

And though hope might start to become brittle,

My faith is surely here to stay.

The storms got nothing on me,

I've come to enjoy it's roar.
