Her story wasn't like Cinderella's,

Lips adorned with a silver spoon were hers.

Locked in a mansion, covered with wealth's umbrella.

She didn't have a stepmom to fear.

But, alone was she with walls so high.

A fake smile decorated her succulent lips.

With pride from baseless energy supplies.
