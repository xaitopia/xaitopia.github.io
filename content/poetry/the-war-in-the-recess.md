---
title: "The War in the Recess"
date: 2021-06-05
draft: false
categories: ["Poetry"]
---

The day is young with thoughts,

Exhausted from last night's battles.

A battle not tattooed on memories,

Has come to be a burden.

Locked in the recess of consciousness,

The real battle rages.

Though the consciousness denies the fact,

Still the war rages.
