When they called your name,

I came apart at the seams —

not from sadness,

but from joy so large

it could not stay inside my body.

 

I have never clapped so hard

for anything that was not mine.

And that is the gift of you:

you made me understand

that love has no scoreboard,

no ledger of victories,

no secret tally of whose turn it is to shine.

 

Your win arrived

and I felt it

like a door flung open

in a room I thought was sealed.

 

More light came in.

More than I expected.

More than I deserved.

 

I was there. I saw it.

And I will carry the memory

of your face in that moment

longer than you will.
