---
title: "Taste of Vinegar"
date: 2021-05-20
draft: false
tags: ["Memory", "Bitterness", "Acceptance"]
categories: ["Poetry"]
description: "On the way memory turns sour when you fight it — emotions that taste of vinegar only when resisted."
author: "Xai"
---

Memories dance within me,

Raising within, forgotten emotions.

And once there's resistance in me,

The emotions take on the taste of vinegar.
