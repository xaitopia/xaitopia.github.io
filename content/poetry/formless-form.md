---
title: "Formless Form"
date: 2021-06-05
draft: false
categories: ["Poetry"]
---

The energy to move is lacking.

The desire for more so slacking.

With little magnitude and no direction,

I have come to be a scalar quantity.

Drifting down the sands of time,

Locked in this fabric of space.

A space that squeezes the essence from my soul,

I have come to make peace with this war.
