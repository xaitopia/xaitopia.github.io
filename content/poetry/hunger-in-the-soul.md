title: "Hunger in the Soul"
date: 2021-05-17
draft: false
tags: ["Desire", "Emptiness", "Soul"]
categories: ["Poetry"]
description: "On the particular hunger that lives deeper than appetite — the ache that no thing outside you fills."
author: "Xai"

The rumble vibrates through tissues,

As desires sends painful pleasures

Through a helpless being.

Powerless against the emptiness,

That comes from the hunger in my soul.
