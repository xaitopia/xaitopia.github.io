---
title: "Old Squeaky Chair"
date: 2021-05-16
draft: false
categories: ["Poetry"]
---

Salient smiles from pertinent faces.

Letting love flow through channels of hope.

All that would remain after this journey,

Would be memories of a life well spent.

While we sit alone, rocking that old squeaky chair.

Patiently waiting for an end to an amazing life.
