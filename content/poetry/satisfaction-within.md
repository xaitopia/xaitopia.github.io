---
title: "Satisfaction Within"
date: 2021-05-24
draft: false
tags: ["Inner peace", "Acceptance", "Self"]
categories: ["Poetry"]
description: "On releasing the search for satisfaction outside yourself — the peace that comes from looking inward."
author: "Xai"
---

Seize the longing.

Seize the expectations.

Let go of the fear,

Shake off the anxiety.

For nothing outside of you,

Would give to you

The satisfaction you crave.

Make peace with this,

And peace would be yours.
