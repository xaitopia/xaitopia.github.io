title: "As If No Time"

We find each other on a Tuesday afternoon,

ten years dissolved between the first hello and now.

You laugh the same — that crooked, sudden bloom —

and we are eight years old again, somehow.

 

Ten years dissolved between the first hello and now,

a decade folded small, slipped in a coat.

And we are eight years old again, somehow,

the old language returning to the throat.

 

A decade folded small, slipped in a coat,

the years like water that refused to age us.

The old language returning to the throat,

the story starting fresh upon the same pages.

 

The years like water that refused to age us —

we find each other on a Tuesday afternoon.

The story starting fresh upon the same pages.

You laugh the same — that crooked, sudden bloom.
