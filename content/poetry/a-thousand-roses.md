---
title: "A Thousand Roses"
date: 2021-01-21
draft: false
categories: ["Poetry"]
---

I know of a thousand roses,

Scents that were out of this world.

I know of harmonious sounds,

Sounds nowhere near this dimension.

A goddess among peers,

With a thousand rose for a scent,

And harmonious sounds for laughter.

I know of a woman.
