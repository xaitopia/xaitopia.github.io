---
title: "My Drowning Shadow"
date: 2021-03-06
draft: false
categories: ["Poetry"]
---

Neck deep in lust, without a direction.

There's no will to satisfy this lust.

Yet it continues to grow ever strong,

it creeps into me offering me powers that blinds me.

Deep in love, without the power to accept the pain.

There's no will to accept this part.

Yet it continues to stab me with increased consistency,

it creeps into me, offering feelings of helplessness.

Deep in myself, without a map to call my own.

There's no strength to make my own.

Yet the need continues to eat at me.

It soaks me with feelings of unrest, I know not where to start.
