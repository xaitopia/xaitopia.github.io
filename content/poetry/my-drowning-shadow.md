---
title: "My Drowning Shadow"
date: 2021-03-06
draft: false
tags: ["Desire", "Conflict", "Shadow self"]
categories: ["Poetry"]
description: "A reckoning with the parts of the self that consume without satisfying — lust, longing, and the pain of acceptance."
author: "Xai"
---

Neck deep in lust, without a direction.

There is no will to satisfy this lust.

Yet it continues to grow ever strong,

it creeps into me offering powers that blinds me.

Deep in love, without the power to accept the pain.

There is no will to accept this part.

Yet it continues to stab me with increased consistency.
