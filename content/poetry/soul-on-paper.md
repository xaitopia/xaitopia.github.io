---
title: "Soul on Paper"
date: 2021-05-24
draft: false
tags: ["Writing", "Emptiness", "Soul"]
categories: ["Poetry"]
description: "When the only direction left is the page — picking up a pen to stain paper with what remains of the soul."
author: "Xai"
---

Drained of essence,

With no substance.

On a lonely path,

That sets me apart.

Without a direction,

With no discretion.

I have picked a pen,

To stain this paper with my soul.
