---
title: "Dancing in Harmony"
date: 2021-05-24
draft: false
categories: ["Poetry"]
---

Play me tunes of peace.

Sing me lullabies so sweet.

Let's dance with great abandonment,

For who knows when this will end.

Dance with me lover,

Let's move legs to the tones of this play.

We will dance with the universe,

Moving between absolute polarities.
