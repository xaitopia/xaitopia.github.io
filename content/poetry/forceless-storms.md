description: "On the weight of storms that leave no mark — and the quiet certainty that the sun will take its true form."

The soundless noise from forceless storms,

Drive waveless ripple down flaccid spines.

The vertical stance now a job of stress.

Allow the horizon become parallel with us.

The sun would still one day take its true form.

Until the sky takes the color of peace,

And the light retires the darkness to sleep.
