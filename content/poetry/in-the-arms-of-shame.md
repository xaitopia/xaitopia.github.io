In need of acceptance,

When the only acceptance she needed was her own.

In search for love,

When the truest love could only be gotten from her soul.

In search for happiness,

If only she knew she had it in her all along.

So she found herself in the arm of shame.

Creating holes in her dignity with bullets of low self worth,

She let herself fall in line with the gutter rats.

Oh, if only she knew a throne was vacant waiting for her to just awoken.

A girl with the eyes of a goddess,

A woman with a heart of gold,

Oh, she knew not what she's got.

Yet her kingdom waits patiently for

When the Queen would wake.
