---
title: "In the Arms of Shame"
date: 2023-01-27
draft: false
tags: ["Identity", "Self-worth", "Women"]
categories: ["Poetry"]
description: "A woman searching outward for what only lives within — love, acceptance, happiness she already held."
author: "Xai"

In need of acceptance,

When the only acceptance she needed was her own.

In search for love,

When the truest love could only be gotten from her soul.

In search for happiness,

If only she knew she had it in her all along.

So she found herself in the arm of shame.
