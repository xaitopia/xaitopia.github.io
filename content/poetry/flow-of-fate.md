---
title: "Flow of Fate"
date: 2021-05-29
draft: false
categories: ["Poetry"]
---

We can't force the flow of fate,

All we have to offer is absolute faith.

For when the darkness starts to reign,

Peace wets our mind like September rain.

Like the gracious dancer we'll move,

Letting the peace that comes with faith hit us in colossal moves.
