---
title: "Masks of Old and New"
date: 2021-05-21
draft: false
categories: ["Poetry"]
---

I have been running,

Running so fast from me.

Taking on masks of old.

Taking on masks of new.

Hiding behind shadows unknown.

Denying the me that remains raw.

Drowning the self whose identity lies in unwanted purpose.

But time is a force so powerful.

And this me continues to grow.

It's starting to seem obvious,

That the real me has started to show.
