The zipper's teeth are clenched against the past,

My childhood city calls me through the glass.

This heavy weight is meant to hold and last,

I watch the shadows of the suburbs pass.

 

My childhood city calls me through the glass,

The platform hums a frantic, rising tune.

I watch the shadows of the suburbs pass,

I'll wake beneath a different kind of moon.

 

The platform hums a frantic, rising tune,

Determined hands have packed the years away.

I'll wake beneath a different kind of moon,

I'm finished with the ghosts of yesterday.

 

Determined hands have packed the years away,

This heavy weight is meant to hold and last.

I'm finished with the ghosts of yesterday;

The zipper's teeth are clenched against the past.
