About

Emmanuel Chukwu

A storyteller working across fiction, poetry, and nonfiction.

Writing under the name Xai, I make work about the moments that hold a life together — grief that transforms into stars, love that bends like galaxies, the silence between two people that says everything. My fiction, poetry, and essays are rooted in place and memory, shaped by the belief that the most universal stories are always the most specific ones.
